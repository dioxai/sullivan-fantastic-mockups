# Direction G — The Marquee

A cinematic, movie-studio reel posture adapted to a luxury host. The homepage is a full-bleed three-slot rotating carousel — Sullivan's YouTube promo (muted, looped, scaled to crop the YT chrome), cross-faded with two stage stills every six seconds via a pure CSS keyframe animation. There are no player controls, no audio, no chrome — just movement under a navy gradient with a huge Cormorant italic "Sullivan Fantastic" wordmark, the tagline "the reel of a thousand rooms," an outlined-pill CTA, and three animated dots marking the active slide. Below the marquee the page settles into a deep-navy × electric-champagne × oxblood palette: a narrow italic prologue, a five-card horizontal Engagements scroll, a three-column Bill (Sullivan Fantastic / Jackson Set / Private Evening), a Behind-the-Marquee about block with a generously cropped portrait (`object-position: center 20%` everywhere, no head crops), a full-bleed italic pull-quote framed by an oxblood opening quote glyph, a generously-spaced numbered "Three Acts" section (We Talk → We Design → The Curtain Rises, each in its own card with 70px internal padding), a two-column inquiry form with placeholder contacts, and a sledgehammer footer wordmark. Pricing is hidden; all CTAs route to inquiry. Responsive from 390px to 1920px, single file, only Google Fonts as an external dependency.
