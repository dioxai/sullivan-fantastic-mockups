# Direction C — Atelier Concierge · Memo

This mockup positions Chris as a private host running a small, members-only atelier — Soho House warmth crossed with a jazz-lounge intimacy, not the cold marble of "luxury." The palette is anchored in deep forest green (#0F1E1A) with oxblood gradients and brass type accents, finished in warm cream body copy, and the chrome is built from frosted glass-morphism cards that subtly lift on hover. Fraunces (in its display and italic cuts) carries every headline and serif body block, while Inter handles labels, eyebrows, and UI — keeping the voice literary but never stiff. The hero opens with a curtain-reveal animation over a warm-lit photo of Chris, an "A Personal Invitation" eyebrow, and a "From Chris Sullivan, your host" sign-off; the page then moves through a first-person letter signed "— Chris," three frosted Salon cards written as intimate paragraphs (not bullet points), a quiet trio of "moments," a "Who I Host" portrait block, a three-step *We talk → We design the evening → I host* process, and a concierge form (name, email, textarea, "Begin"). The footer closes on "Reachable directly. By appointment." — the whole site reads less like a vendor pitch and more like a handwritten note from someone who clearly already has a calendar.
