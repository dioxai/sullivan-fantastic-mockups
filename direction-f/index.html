— The Headliner —

There are entertainers.
And there is a host.

Sullivan Fantastic is a live act — a room-filler built for listening rooms, side-stages, private evenings, and the occasional black-tie gala. Blues, soul, storytelling. Theatrical instincts, serious chops. One voice carrying the night. No costume piece, no karaoke, no press-play-and-pray. A live act that reads the room and responds — and leaves the guests talking long after the last glass is cleared.
