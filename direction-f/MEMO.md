# Direction F — The Headliner

Direction F translates the Chris Brown World Store's tonal-monochrome posture into a luxury entertainment company. The whole page lives inside one warm amber temperature — tuxedo black `#0E0B08`, champagne gold `#D4B26A`, ivory `#F1ECE0`, and a deep cognac `#704423` mid-tone — so every portrait is filtered (`sepia(0.12) contrast(1.05) saturate(0.95)`) to dissolve into the palette rather than pop off it. The hero is a horizontal 1/3–2/3 split: SULLIVAN set huge in Cormorant Garamond Bold (clamped 72–260px) lives on the left with a champagne eyebrow, italic tagline, and a single "Inquire →" link; the right two-thirds is a full-bleed editorial portrait pinned at `object-position: center 20%` so heads never crop. Below: a cognac marquee, an italic "There are entertainers. And there is a host." statement with a gold drop-cap, three champagne-bordered service cards on cognac, a 12-item italic engagements list in two columns, two horizontal photo spreads with italic captions, a warm pull-quote, a quiet inquire block with placeholder contact details and a Calendly-style CTA pointing to `#inquire`, and a footer with the SULLIVAN FANTASTIC wordmark blown out. Pricing stays hidden. Responsive from 390px (single-column hero, stacked grid, single-column list) up to 1920px (wordmark scales to 260px). Reads as "the headliner has arrived" — his name is the story.
