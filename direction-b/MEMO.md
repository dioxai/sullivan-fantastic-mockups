# Direction B — Editorial Magazine · MEMO

Direction B treats Sullivan Fantastic as a *Vogue / Robb Report* feature spread rather than a vendor page: Chris is the cover talent of "Vol. MMXXVI · No. 1 — The Gentleman Host Issue," not a booking listing. The palette is warm ivory (`#F8F4EC`) with deep ink text, antique gold (`#B8956A`) for tracked-out eyebrows and rules, and oxblood (`#5B1A1A`) reserved for the italic pull-quote and select display italics — the page reads like printed paper. Playfair Display carries 120px+ cover headlines, drop-caps, and italic decks against Inter's tiny tracked uppercase eyebrows; motion is intentionally absent so the drama is white space and typography. Structure follows the brief exactly: masthead lockup with hairline rules → magazine cover hero with portrait (Plate I) and credits → drop-cap editor's letter → asymmetric photo grid (one hero plate + five smaller plates pulled from Chris's real IG/TikTok assets) → "The Repertoire" three-column section (Private Events / Corporate & Galas / Weddings) with eyebrows + lush copy → dark featured spread with a single image and editorial caption → oxblood italic pull-quote attributed to "A Host's Note, Aspen" → "Notable Engagements" set as a numbered editorial list (Private Estate in Aspen, Charity Gala, Birthday at the Met, etc.) → "For private inquiry" two-column spread pairing concierge contact lines with a bordered card CTA → magazine footer with masthead reprise, hairline rules, and "Vol. MMXXVI · No. 1." Existing brand vocabulary is preserved ("reads the house and responds," "48 hours for serious rooms," "by introduction") while the voice is pushed firmly editorial — discreet, lightly archival, and decisively positioned as the gentleman host the room remembers.
